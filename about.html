Our Story

Founded in 2024, NetSec Academy started with a simple mission: to make high-quality network engineering education accessible to everyone. We noticed that traditional courses were too theoretical, leaving students unprepared for actual industry challenges.

Today, we offer a hands-on, simulated environment where students can break things safely and learn how to fix them.
